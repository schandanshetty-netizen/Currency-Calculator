Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

At Currency Converter (rupeerate.duckdns.org), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our currency conversion service.

TL;DR: We don't collect or store personal information. All conversions happen in your browser. We only use localStorage for your preferences (favorites) and may show ads.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

We do NOT collect or store:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses (unless you contact us)
  • Payment information
  • Conversion amounts or currency pairs
  • Transaction history

2. Automatically Collected Information

Our service may automatically collect:

  • IP-based Location: Used only to auto-detect your currency (not stored)
  • Browser Type & Version: For compatibility purposes
  • Device Information: To optimize display across devices
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous analytics via Google AdSense

3. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save:

  • Favorite Currencies: Your starred currency pairs for quick access
  • These are stored locally on YOUR device only and never sent to our servers
  • You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings

How We Use Information

The minimal information we process is used to:

  • Provide accurate currency conversion services
  • Auto-detect your local currency for convenience
  • Display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • Analyze traffic patterns (anonymously)

Third-Party Services

Currency Data Providers

We fetch exchange rates from:

  • Frankfurter API: European Central Bank official data
  • Exchange Rates API: Global market rates

These services may log API requests but do NOT receive personal information from us.

Location Detection

We use ipapi.co to detect your country/currency based on IP address. This is a one-time request per session and is not stored on our servers.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Show relevant ads based on your browsing history
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

You can control ad personalization through Google Ad Settings.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses:

  • Essential Storage: localStorage for saving your favorite currencies
  • Third-Party Cookies: Google AdSense may set cookies for advertising purposes

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit some functionality.

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • No server-side storage of conversion data
  • Client-side processing for all conversions
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Protection against common web vulnerabilities

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold (which is minimal)
  • Deletion: Clear your localStorage favorites anytime
  • Opt-Out: Disable ads using browser extensions or settings
  • Contact Us: Ask questions about privacy at any time

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

International Users

Our service is available globally. By using it, you consent to processing data in accordance with this policy, regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  • Contact Form: Contact Page

Consent
