Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your exchange rates accurate?
A: Yes, we source rates from official financial institutions (Frankfurter API, Exchange Rates API) and update them in real-time.

Q: Is this service free?
A: Absolutely! Our currency converter is 100% free to use with no hidden charges or premium tiers.

Q: Can I use this for commercial purposes?
A: Yes, businesses are welcome to use our tool. For API access or integration, please contact us.

Q: How often are rates updated?
A: Exchange rates are fetched in real-time from our data providers whenever you perform a conversion.

Q: Do you support Indian Rupee conversions?
A: Yes! We have a dedicated section showing INR exchange rates for 10 popular currencies with percentage changes.
